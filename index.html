Our Heritage

Where Ancient Craft
Meets Royal Ambition

Founded in the heart of Old Dubai, Razzal Fragrance was born from a singular vision: to preserve the ancient art of Arabian perfumery and elevate it to the heights of modern luxury. For nearly five decades, our master perfumers have walked the spice markets, forest paths, and sacred valleys of the world in search of the purest raw materials.

We believe a fragrance is not merely a scent — it is a memory, a landscape, a story told across generations. Each bottle carries within it the warmth of the majlis, the silence of the desert at dawn, and the hospitality of a people whose generosity is written into their very culture.

The Finest Materials

Signature Ingredients

Indian Oud

Assam, India

The rarest form of agarwood, prized for its deep, animalistic richness and centuries of ceremonial significance.

Cambodian Oud

Kampong Speu

Lighter and more floral than its Indian counterpart, bearing luminous sweetness over a woody heart.

Taif Rose

Taif, Saudi Arabia

Harvested once a year at dawn, the Taif rose yields an oil of incomparable honeyed depth and spice.

Amber

Gulf & Baltic

Ancient resinous warmth with deep, honeyed notes that form the backbone of our most treasured blends.

White Musk

Synthesized Pure

Clean, celestial, and skin-close — our white musk creates a luminous trail that lasts from morning to midnight.

Frankincense

Oman & Somalia

The sacred resin of the Boswellia tree, offering a transcendent, crystalline smokiness revered for millennia.

Saffron

Kashmir & Iran

Liquid gold from the mountains — lending rich, leathery warmth and a precious spice that transforms every formula.

Sandalwood

Mysore, India

Creamy, meditative, and enduring — Mysore sandalwood is the soul of balance in our most beloved compositions.

المجلس The Majlis Experience

Every fragrance tells a story of hospitality, heritage and timeless Arabian elegance

In the Arab world, scent is the language of welcome. A host burns oud before guests arrive. Rose water is poured over hands. Incense circulates through the room — a gesture that says: you are seen, you are honoured, you belong here. Our fragrances carry this spirit into every moment of modern life.
